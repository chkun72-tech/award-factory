Handyman Quote-to-Follow-up Autopilot

An AI agent workflow that turns messy customer repair enquiries into structured job scopes, quote drafts, customer replies, and follow-up tasks.

Customer enquiry

Hi, my bathroom tap is leaking and I may also need a cabinet fixed. Can someone come this week? I am in Parramatta.
Intake parser Service classifier Missing-info checker Quote draft Reply draft Follow-up task

Structured job scope

  • Service: plumbing + general handyman
  • Location: Parramatta
  • Urgency: medium; may become urgent if leak is active
  • Visit window: this week

Missing information

  • Photo/video of tap leak
  • Leak severity and whether water can be isolated
  • Cabinet issue details and photos
  • Preferred access times

Owner next action

Ask for photos, confirm whether the leak is active, then offer an inspection window before sending a final quote.

Quote draft

Draft only: call-out / inspection plus repair estimate. Final pricing depends on photos, parts, access, and site inspection.

Customer reply draft

Thanks for reaching out. Could you please send a photo or short video of the leaking tap and the cabinet issue? Also let us know your preferred time this week and whether the leak is still active.

Follow-up task

Follow up in 24 hours if no photos are received. Owner approval required before sending quote or customer message.
Safety boundary: this agent prepares drafts and tasks. It does not automatically send customer messages or make binding quotes.
