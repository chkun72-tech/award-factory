# AF-003｜Recording Narration Script

Use this while recording the 3-minute demo video.

## 0:00–0:15

Today I am showing Handyman Quote-to-Follow-up Autopilot, an AI agent workflow for small local service businesses.

The problem is simple: customer enquiries are messy, incomplete, and easy to lose.

## 0:15–0:35

Here is an example customer message.

A customer says their bathroom tap is leaking, they may need a cabinet fixed, they want someone this week, and they are in Parramatta.

For a busy owner, this still requires several steps: classify the job, ask missing questions, prepare a quote draft, reply to the customer, and remember to follow up.

## 0:35–1:15

Now I run the autopilot.

The agent turns the raw message into a structured job scope. It identifies plumbing plus general handyman work, extracts the location, detects the urgency, and notes that this may become urgent if the leak is active.

## 1:15–1:45

Next, it checks what information is missing.

Before preparing a final quote, the owner needs photos, leak severity, cabinet details, and access times.

This keeps the workflow practical and safe.

## 1:45–2:15

The agent then prepares useful business outputs:

a quote draft, a customer reply draft, and a follow-up task.

These are drafts only. The owner still reviews and approves them before anything is sent.

## 2:15–2:40

Technically, this is an agent pipeline.

Qwen can power extraction, classification, and draft generation.

Alibaba Cloud can store job records, agent logs, and follow-up tasks, or host the demo service.

## 2:40–3:00

The key idea is not to replace the business owner.

The key idea is to reduce repetitive admin work so the owner can respond faster, quote more consistently, and follow up reliably.

This is a small, focused AI agent for a real operational workflow.
